FAQs

+ Where do you operate?

Answer - We operate in Cebu City and LapuLapu City catering cities in between up to Consolacion, Liloan and down to Basak, Pardo.

+ Where is your actual store?

Answer - We do not have any actual store yet. We only deliver for now.

+ When do you open or close?

Answer - We are open Monday to Friday between 10 am to 6 pm only.

+ How Do I Pay?

Answer - When you order, you will be provided a selection to either via COD, GCash, PayMaya or Bank Transfer. COD is only available when you order for yourself.

When paying via GCash or PayMay or Bank Transfer, you will be contacted by our Cashier to finalise the delivery fee and get the payment details.

+ Can I order for my family or friends?

Answer -Yes, you can. When ordering, you will be asked if it is for your or for others. You just need to provide the recipient’s contact name, number and address when ordering.

+ I am planning to go home, how long do your products last?

Answer - Our products are frozen except for the Lettuce & Rice. They will last for more than 3 months as long as they kept frozen. Once in transit, kept them cool and they stay fresh for more than one hour.

Rice are cooked and kept warm for the day. Lettuce are kept with their roots and will only be plucked once an order is ready for packaging and delivery.

+ What is your standard delivery time?

Answer -We prepare your meals fresh so allow us to gather and assemble your order/s for 40-50 minutes from order confirmation.

+ How is the delivery fee calculated & who pays them?

Answer -The delivery fee are paid by the customer. And the fee reflected on your invoice are estimated delivery fees only and may differ by +-10% from the actual booking.

We use MAXIM for all COD orders but we could use Angkas or Lalamove when you pay via GCash, PayMay or Bank Transfer.

+ Can we cancel our order?

Answer -No, you cannot cancel your order once you confirmed and an invoice is generated.

+ Can I ask for a refund?

Answer -Unfortunately, No. We do not process refunds. We are a small business operting solely on trust and confidence. We make sure the foods we deliver are fresh.
